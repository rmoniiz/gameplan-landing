Behind the platform

Created from a real coaching need

I am a coach certified by the Brazilian Football Confederation (CBF – License C), with 8 years of experience working across different environments and countries. Throughout this journey, I identified a recurring problem — both in my own workflow and among other coaches: either coaches rely on multiple disconnected tools to manage their work, or they lose control over what truly matters — training sessions, tactical principles and player development.

This real, day-to-day challenge is what led me to build GamePlan: a more structured, clear and efficient way to manage the entire coaching process in one system.

What it improves day to day

  • Control over what has been trained
  • Organization of a personal session database
  • Comparison between training and match reality
  • Tracking real player development
