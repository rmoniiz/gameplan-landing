Behind the tool

Created from the real routine of coaching, observing and adjusting

I am a CBF licensed coach with 8 years of experience in different contexts and countries. Along the way, I noticed a clear pattern: coaches need a cleaner way to organize exercises, principles, players and match feedback inside one process.

That practical need led me to create GamePlan: a tool to structure coaching work with more clarity, consistency and continuity.

What improves day to day

  • Control of what was trained
  • Organization of your own exercise database
  • Comparison between training and match
  • Player development tracking
