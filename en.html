About us

Built by someone who lived the pitch before building the product.

My relationship with football started long before any platform. My grandfather, Adão, was an amateur football coach, and one idea from him stayed with me: running without intelligence can hurt the team more than it helps.

For him, football was also about cooperation, joy and belonging. The boys had to play well, feel good and leave the pitch wanting to come back. After wins, everyone shared a crate of soda. That picture shaped how I see the game.

I started my coaching journey in 2017, working in football summer camps in the United States. Since then, I have lived different countries, clubs and football cultures. When I moved to the Netherlands, I felt the need to improve my process even more: keep better records, recover older ideas and understand more clearly whether training was showing up on match day.

GamePlan was born from that path. Not to replace the coach’s eye, but to give more structure to what coaches already build every day.
